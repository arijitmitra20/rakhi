Savan main aaya rakhi ka tevhar
Bahen ki khushi main bhai ki khushi hai
Dekho dono main kitna hai payar



Sabse pyara mera bhai
Sukh dukh main sath rahena
Jeewan ki khushiyan hai tumse
Tum ho to phir kya kahena



Sawan ki rimjhim phuwar hai
Raksha bandhan ka tevhar hai
Bhai bahen ki mithi si takrar
Aisa yah pyara hai khushiyon ka tevhar

bhai bahen ka yah pyara rishta hai

mathe par chamakne wala roli aur chandan
pyar se mithai khilao pyari bahen
dekh ise chalak uthi aankhain bhar aaya man

mile bhai ko khushiya hazar
